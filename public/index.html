Through mentorship, mentees will engage in the program to expand on their current skill set, knowledge, and educational background. By engaging with seasoned professionals mentees can gain insight into the secrets of success in their first role. Subjects our mentors provide advisory on are:

Technology Development

  • Data management
  • Block chain and Web3 development

Tech Leadership

  • Project Management
  • Technical Guidance

  • Digital marketing

A network of support and wisdom

Navigating the job industry is a daunting task for anyone trying to step into their first role and we strive to see everyone succeed!

Join an inclusive community of juniors, seniors and recent graduates who are looking to gain an advantage by learning from experienced professionals who have “been there and done that”, and are willing to share their life experiences.

Career Development

Mentees will have access to industry professionals with decades of experience in tech industry through:

  • Fostering a personal network
  • Expanding on foundational knowledge
  • Increasing confidence and communicaiton skills

Support

As you are taking your first step into the industry, we all need support and to feel advocated as the experience can be overwhelming. Support we provide are:

  • Advisory on career path
  • Address challenges mentees face
  • Provide feedback and focus on goals

Practical Application

Learn from successful professionals who already navigated through the industry. Our mentors help expand on foundational knowledge through:

  • One on one sessions
  • Learning activities
  • Mock interviews

Paying it forward

The technology domain is changing at a rapid pace and technology oriented graduates may not be stepping into their first role with confidence on how to be successful in a corporate environment.

As seasoned technology executives,you have a wealth of insight gained over the years. By sharing key insights and knowledge with students approaching graduation, they will be better prepared in achieveing success in their first job.

Personal Fulfillment

As a mentor fostering and promoting an environment of growth, it is rewarding to see a mentee prosper under your guidance through:

  • Offering encouragement and positive reinforcement
  • Providing guidance and constructive feedback
  • Providing industry experience to mentees

Expanding Network

As a mentor, not only will you have access to a vetted pool of candidates but also other like minded mentors.

  • Meet other mentors who enjoy paying it forward
  • Growing a personal network of mentees and other tech executives
  • Build confidence through enhanced communication and leadership skills

New Perspectives

Learning never stops even with decades of experience. Taking on the role of a mentor offers a chance to gain new perspectives and experiences from mentees of diverse backgrounds through:

  • Self reflecting on your experiences
  • Connecting with someone of a different background
  • Boosts interpersonal skills and knowledge
